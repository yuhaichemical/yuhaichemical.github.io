Introduction

Established in 2001, Guang Zhou Yuhai Liye Chemical Co., Ltd wholly owned by Best Trade Industrial Limited in Hong Kong is a high-technology enterprise with its production base in China Guangzhou. It specializes in manufacturing U.V varnish, water based varnish, water based adhesive for paper printing and packaging industry.

Nowadays, Yuhai Liye has developed to be a leading chemical enterprise in China combined with research and study, manufacturing and marketing. Our customers are distributed in China mainland, Hong Kong, Southeast Asia, Middle East, Europe, USA and other places. High quality products and professional service from Yu Hai Liye are warmly welcomed by customers worldwide.

We have our own professional R&D and production team and closely cooperate with many high technology study and research institute in China recent years. The most advanced and up to date new technologies are introduced into our production procedure continuously. We can manufacture specified products according to customer’s special requirement to meet the market demand.

We have advanced and complete manufacturing and testing devices. The manufacturing flow from purchasing raw material to finished products is under strict quality control and inspection, which guarantees our products’ quality and greatly improve its competition in market. Now our product range has entered into UV varnish, water based varnish, base coating varnish for water base, UV varnish for silk screen printing, water based OPP adhesive, adhesive for carton and box etc. All of our products are strictly inspected and tested by National Quality Control Bureau and International Testing and Inspection Institute. The quality completely reached and passed over international standard requirement for environment protection.

With the great improvement of our quality and production capacity continuously, we passed ISO9001：2008 International Quality Management System Certificate and make great achievement in developing new products and field, which spread fame in the same industry.

By choosing to work with Yuhai Liye, you have selected the most current coating technology available for your printing needs. We will go on providing products with excellent quality, sincerely and professional service and create more prosperous blueprint with you in the future together.
